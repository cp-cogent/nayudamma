Dr. Yelavarthy Nayudamma Science and Technology Foundation, Tenali

 

 

Dr.Yelavarthy Nayudamma
(1922-1985)

Dr. Y. Nayudamma Memorial Trust, founded in 1986, seeks to perpetuate the memory of Dr. Nayudamma, one of India's most distinguished scientists, who died in an air crash over the Atlantic in 1985. The Trust confers the Dr. Nayudamma Memorial Award and organises a lecture by the award recipient every year. The award recipients over the years include:Dr. Sam Pitroda, Dr. G.Madhavan Nair,Dr. K. Harinarayana, Dr. V.K.Aatre, Dr. R. Chidambaram, Dr. R.A. Mashelkar, Prof. J.S. Bajaj, Dr. K. Kasturirangan, Dr. Verghese Kurien, Dr. S.Z. Qasim, Prof. M.G.K. Menon, Dr. M.S. Swaminathan, Dr. M.R. Naidu and Dr. U.V. Warlu. It also conducts extension lectures by eminent scientists to expose the lay public to the latest developments in science and technology. Tenali, the hometown of Nayudamma, belonging to Yelavarru village, is the venue for the Trust activities.
